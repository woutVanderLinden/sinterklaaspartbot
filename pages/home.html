I'm PartBot, a Bot that's responsible for managing a couple rooms. most notably Board Games.

How to: Use me!

If you're coming from Pokemon Showdown; you might be familiar with Bots (who am I kidding; you've probably used them often). As a Bot on PS, pretty much all commands directed to me start with my command prefix:
,
. Yeah, the comma. There's actually an interesting story about why it's that; but g'luck finding it.

Anyways, back to business. The commands that'll probably prove the most useful are:
,help
and
,commands
. My Help command offers helpful assistance on every single one of my commands (well, assistance is guaranteed, but it's not always helpful), while Commands allows you to view all the commands that you can use on PartBot.

What else can I do?

Of course, this isn't even close to the limits of my abilities. Among other features, I support a multitude of games, and have some impressive utility and leisure commands. Most of my games were developed for Board Games, which regularly hosts games of Chess, Othello, Chain Reaction, Exploding Voltorb, and some of my other games. Of course, I also have more epic features - my quotes system is perhaps the most advanced on PS, with elegant displays and intuitive commands. If you get stuck, you can always use the
,help q
command to get some assistance.

Apart from these, I have some basic utility commands (
,pick
,
,timer
, and
,age
to mention a few), and I support some epic leaderboards and room shops. Sadly, these can't be configured without an administrator at the moment, but plans are in motion for enhanced configuration.

I also have some interesting mental math games, and support cool features like joinphrases and skewering people with chopsticks. If you ever want to try, contact PartMan! (He's the nerd that wrote me.)

The Hidden World - PMs

Believe it or not, I can do stuff in PMs, too. Apart from most of the regular utility commands, I have a couple interesting commands that can only be used in PMs. Prime examples of these are the
,filtername
and
,filtermoves
commands; but I have some top-secret commands that do epic stuff and can't be accessed by regulars. :3 I also sometimes talk if I'm bored; so don't be surprised if I PM you out of the blue if I need to talk.
